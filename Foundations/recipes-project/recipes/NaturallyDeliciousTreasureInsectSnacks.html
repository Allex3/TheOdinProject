Naturally Delicious Treasure Insect Snacks

Description

A group of delicious snacks prepared by Senshi, made entirely of different types of Treasure Insects. Crackers made from Coin Bugs and Pearl Centipede skewers accompany a jam made from a Treasure Insect nest.

Dishes

Treasure Insect Nest Jam

Ingredients

  • Treasure insect nest (eggs and larvae removed)
  • Water (50 ml)
  • Sugar (if needed)

Coin Bug Crackers

Ingredients

  • Coin bugs (as many as needed)
  • Oil (to taste)
  • Salt (to taste)

Pearl Centipede Skewers

Ingredients

  • Pearl centipedes (2)
  • Salt (to taste)
