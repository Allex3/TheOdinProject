Jack-o'-Lantern Potage and Sauteed Dryad Buds with Cheese

Description

A nourishing dish made by Senshi to refresh and rejuvenate the young members of the Touden party. Dryad fruit is softened by simmering in water then mashed and seasoned to make a potage. Dryad buds are sliced crosswise and fried gently in butter with some mushrooms then covered in melted cheese and served.

Ingredients

  • Dryad fruit (1 head)
  • Mandrake (as desired; 1)
  • Dryad buds (2)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
